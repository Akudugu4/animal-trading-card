Rabbit

A rabbit's powerful hind legs and sharp senses help it escape predators in the wild.

  • Scientific Name:Oryctolagus cuniculus
  • Average Length: 40 centemeters
  • Average Lifespan: 9 years
  • Habitat: Grasslands, forests, and meadows

Covered in soft fur with long ears and a twitching nose, rabbits are quick and quiet creatures. They grow to about 40 centimeters long and are known for their strong legs and digging abilities, often creating burrows to live and hide in.
